Self Driven to Make Self Drives

Who We Are?

We are a group of highly motivated students sharing the dream about a better, safer world with intelligent transportation systems. Our mission is to make self driving vehicles for masses. We are from various branches of science and engineering, ranging from Physics to Mechanical Engineering, from Engineering Design to Electrical Engineering.

What we do?

We are currently working on our first prototype self driving platform. This platform is mainly for use in controlled environment like factory floors, golf courses etc, inside buildings., We are aiming to present this autonomous platform in the prestigious Intelligent Ground Vehicle Competition next year (2017).

Technologies Used

We believe that any great product starts with strong fundamentals in design. We are exploiting the cutting edge developments in technology to make out platform future ready. Ranging from the software stack for robotic platform to frameworks for deep learning, we are always looking out for the best technologies we can use. We uses multiple open source softwares like Linux(Ubuntu), Robot Operating System, OpenCV etc in this platform. We also exploits the simplicity of arduino and capabilities of ARM processors for our electro-mechanical controls. We are looking forward to replace our onboard computer with much powerful GPU supported units.

Where are these useful?

Our aim to create a safe and efficient transportation solutions. Our current prototype is capable of working with humans in a controlled environment like factory floors, warehouses, hospitals, hotels, golf courses etc., Soon we planning to get the platform out for on-road tests.

Other Applications

  • Military Mobility
  • Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS)
  • Manufacturing
  • Hospitals
  • Agriculture
